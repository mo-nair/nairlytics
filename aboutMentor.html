About My Mentor

Neeraj Madan

Neeraj Madan is the Solutions Director of Data and AI at Evergreen. With over 20 years of experience working at IBM prior to this, Neeraj holds the honor of being an IBM Certified AI Expert. He holds 22 accolades, including the IBM Worldwide Organic Growth Ventures Award, for his work at IBM that includes leading AI and ML teams as well as co-authoring the IBM Open Group Data Scientist Certification.


Beyond his professional career, he is an adjunct professor at Collin College's Continuing Education Department for the Data Science Certificate Series. He also serves as the chair of 501(c)(3) non-profit Keep Little Elm Beautiful, Little Elm's official affiliate of Keep Texas Beautiful.
